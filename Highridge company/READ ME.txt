1.Project Title- Weekly Payment System for Highridge Construction Company.

2. Description
Explanation of the project, its purpose, and what it accomplishes

This Python program facilitates weekly payments for workers at Highridge Construction Company. It dynamically generates a list of 400 workers, assigns salary levels based on defined conditions, and produces payment slips for each worker.

3. Features

Dynamically creates a list of 400 workers with random attributes.
Implements conditional logic to categorize employees.
Generates payment slips for each worker.
It includes error handling to manage unexpected issues.
